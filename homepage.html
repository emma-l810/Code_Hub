About THE CODING CLUB

THE CODING CLUB was created during the COVID pandemic to give busy people--who otherwise might not have the time--the opportunity to learn basic programming skills in different computer languages. We hope that people can benefit from the lessons that we have created, and that they will be interested in pursuing more indepth computer science opportunities through their school's programs and extracurricular activities.
